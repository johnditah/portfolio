My Biography

I've achieved my BSc with a major in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Buea Cameroon and a major in Civil Engineering from National Advanced School of Public Works. I've attained over a decade of experience ranging from Staff field assistant to Senior Civil Engineering Technician. Until 2018, I was employed as a Senior Civil Engineering Technician with Regional Delegation of Housing and Urban Development, wherein I supervised the design and building of construction projects. I am now motivated and eager to apply my experience along with my IT skills to impact and empower teams within an organisation to achieve its business goals.
